This is a subscription service to be automatically notified by e-mail when topics change in this *%BASEWEB%* web. This is a convenient service, so you do not have to come back and check all the time if something has changed. To subscribe, please add a bullet with your %SYSTEMWEB%.WikiName in alphabetical order to this list:

Format:
   * [ webname . ] wikiName - SMTP mail address 
   * [ webName . ] wikiName 
   * SMTP mail address 
   * SMTP mail address : topics 
   * [ webname . ] wikiName : topics
